This product is a student-focused rental marketplace for nding PGs, ats, hostels, single
rooms, co-living spaces, and small rental properties near selected colleges. The V1 is focused
on helping students quickly discover nearby housing and helping owners list veried
properties with admin approval before listings go live.
The product will launch as a usable V1 with a web frontend built using Vite + React + Tailwind,
a React Native mobile app, a FastAPI backend, Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Cloudinary
for images, and OpenStreetM ap + Leaet for maps.[cite:29][cite:31][cite:37]
Students searching for housing near colleges often rely on W hatsApp groups, brokers,
scattered listings, and incomplete local references. This makes it hard to compare options by
distance, rent, room sharing type, amenities, and trustworthiness.
Property owners also face a fragmented process. They do not have a simple way to list
college-nearby properties, manage inquiries, update availability, and reach veried student
demand in one place.
The product solves this by creating a college-rst marketplace where users choose a college
rst, then discover rental inventory within a dened radius with structured listing information
and direct owner contact.
Help students nd nearby rental options faster.
